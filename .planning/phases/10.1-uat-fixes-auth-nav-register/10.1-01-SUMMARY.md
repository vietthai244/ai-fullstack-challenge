## What Was Built

Added a `/auth/` URL guard to the axios 401 response interceptor in `frontend/src/lib/apiClient.ts`. The guard fires immediately after `originalRequest._retry = true` and before the `try {}` block that creates the refresh promise — causing any 401 from `/auth/*` endpoints to propagate directly to React Query rather than triggering a token-refresh retry.
